Who is this workshop for?

This workshop is specifically made for YOU if you are new to Ruby on Rails and...

  • You want to implement an idea and build your own website.
  • You want to pursue a career in web development
  • You will soon be applying to or starting at a full time developer bootcamp.
  • You need to better understand the language of your technical team or co-founder
  • You want to add a new tool to your technical arsenal by learning a rapid way of building web applications with Rails

No matter who you are, this workshop will put rocket boosters on your first steps into web development.

In two intense days, you will:

Learn the basics of the Ruby language

Build a real Rails application step-by-step

Adopt a professional workflow using git

Deploy your application to the web using Heroku

By the end of the course, you will be well prepared to take on more advanced web development projects as you continue your education.

How It Works

Preparation

You should have a basic familiarity with HTML and CSS as taught in a typical "Introduction to Web Design" class or book. You will receive some helpful resources to get you started in the weeks leading up to the course as well.

You will need to bring your own laptop computer and install the necessary software beforehand (instructions and troubleshooting will be provided).

During the Class

The course will be taught using a combination of direct lectures, independent exercises and collaborative project work that will give you a chance to apply what you've learned immediately and to learn with other students.

You'll have plenty of chances to ask questions and work directly with the instructor along the way.

Afterwards

We'll get you pointed in the right direction to continue your education, whatever your goals might be, and will provide you with the resources you need to help you on your way.
