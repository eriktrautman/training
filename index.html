Ruby on Rails
Weekend Bootcamp

Stop thinking about it and start building web applications

Sling shot your way into web development with Ruby on Rails during this weekend course offered by prolific teacher and author Daniel Kehoe. Whether you'd like to build your first "real" web application, are preparing yourself for entry to a fulltime developer bootcamp, or are just frustrated with trying to learn on your own, this course will put rocket boosters on your first steps into web development.
In two intense days, you will: * Learn the basics of the Ruby language * Receive step-by-step instruction in how to build a Ruby on Rails application * Follow techniques and practices used by professional web developers * Build and deploy a real Rails application to the web using Heroku By the end of the course, you should be well prepared to take on more advanced web development concepts and projects. You'll be in a great spot to start making a career change, building a web site for your business, or showing your big idea to the world.
About the Instructor: Daniel Kehoe has been passionately educating developers for years. He is the creator of the popular RailsApps project, which offers tutorials and example applications for professional Rails developers. He is a former writer and editor at PC WORLD and NeXTWORLD magazines who has been working with Rails since 2006 as a developer and CTO in multiple startups. He is known for a dynamic and enthusiastic teaching style and a commitment to empowering students to become successful Rails developers.
