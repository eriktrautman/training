Whether you'd like to build your first real web application, are preparing for entry to a fulltime developer bootcamp, or are just frustrated with trying to learn on your own, this course will put rocket boosters on your first steps into web development.

In two intense days, you will:

Learn the basics of the Ruby language

Build a real Rails application step-by-step

Adopt a professional workflow using git

Deploy your application to the web using Heroku

By the end of the course, you should be well prepared to take on more advanced web development concepts and projects. You'll be in a great spot to start making a career change, building a web site for your business, or showing your big idea to the world.

How It Works

Preparation

You should have a basic familiarity with HTML and CSS as taught in a typical "Introduction to Web Design" class or book. You will receive some helpful resources to get you started in the weeks leading up to the course as well.

You will need to bring your own laptop computer and install the necessary software beforehand (instructions and troubleshooting will be provided).

During the Class

The course will be taught using a combination of direct lectures, independent exercises and collaborative project work that will give you a chance to apply what you've learned immediately and to learn with other students.

Afterwards

You will be pointed in the right direction to continue your education, whatever your goals might be, but we'll provide resources to help you on your way.
